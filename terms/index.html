2. Description of the Service

Chronic Friends is a wellness and community app dedicated exclusively to people living with chronic illness. It does not privilege any single "primary" condition — every supported condition is treated equally. Rather than being built around one diagnosis, the app is organized into modules by body system, so that one person can follow one or several conditions at the same time — because living with more than one chronic condition is common. You choose your condition(s) when you set up the app, and it shows you the self-tracking tools that are relevant to you.

The Service is designed to support people living with a wide and growing range of chronic conditions (grouped below by body system, in no order of priority), including but not limited to:

  • Digestive: Crohn's disease, ulcerative colitis, unclassified IBD (IBD-U), microscopic colitis, irritable bowel syndrome (IBS), SIBO, coeliac disease, gastroparesis, and mastocytosis / MCAS.
  • Muscles & joints: rheumatoid arthritis, ankylosing spondylitis, psoriatic arthritis, fibromyalgia, chronic back and neck pain, Ehlers-Danlos syndrome, osteoarthritis, and juvenile idiopathic arthritis (for adults living with it).
  • Eyes: Sjögren's syndrome, Graves' disease, and chronic uveitis / episcleritis.
  • Skin & mucous membranes: psoriasis, vasculitis, severe atopic dermatitis, and discoid lupus.
  • Pelvic health: endometriosis, adenomyosis, and interstitial cystitis.
  • Breathing & heart: chronic asthma, COPD, and chronic hypertension.
  • Mental health: anxiety disorders, major depression, persistent depression (dysthymia), ADHD, alcohol and substance use disorders, and bipolar disorder.
  • Energy, fatigue & nervous system (available to everyone): chronic fatigue (ME/CFS), postural orthostatic tachycardia syndrome (POTS), Hashimoto's / hypothyroidism, chronic migraine, Addison's disease, adrenal insufficiency, and diabetes (type 1 and type 2).
  • Multi-system conditions such as systemic lupus erythematosus (SLE) and multiple sclerosis (MS), which span several of the areas above.

We add support for further conditions over time. Choosing which condition(s) you follow only personalizes which trackers you see — it is not a diagnosis, and Chronic Friends does not provide medical advice.

The Service offers:

  • Community: open posts and private chats between people living with chronic conditions.
  • Self-tracking: daily logging of wellness, symptoms, food, and habits, with tools that help you see your own patterns over time.
  • Access to verified doctors: where available, the ability to request video consultations with accredited healthcare professionals.
  • Wellness and supply-management tools.

We strive to keep the Service available and up to date, but we may modify, suspend, or withdraw features at any time.
